About Kyubee

The primary objective of this project is to facilitate the creation of a comprehensive questions and notes database for various academic courses. Educators can contribute by uploading question papers and notes related to specific topics within their courses which enable students to access and get customized question papers according to their needs.

Aim

Kyubee aims to build a rich repository of questions that can be utilized for educational purposes. This project aims to contribute and enhance the educational experience by providing a unique approach to question paper creation.
Along with a repository for questions of various courses, this project also incorporates various notes for these courses by the faculty educators who provide materials for their specific courses.

Objectives

- Establishing a Centralized Question Paper & Notes Repository
- Foster Collaboration Between Students and Educators
- Enable Efficient Question Curation and Retrieval
- Integrate User Feedback
- Support Question Paper Customization
- Encourage Lifelong Learning
